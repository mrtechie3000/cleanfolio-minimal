I'm your average nerd with a deep passion for computers and video games.

But it wasn’t until I discovered the limitless potential of PC gaming that my curiosity about computers really took off.

Since then, I’ve been disassembling, building, and repairing PCs, laptops, and other tech devices—combining my love of electronics with the knowledge I’ve gained over the years.
I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA) and am currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science, continually striving to expand my understanding of both hardware and software.
